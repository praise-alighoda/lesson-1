How to make chin chin

Today i will be show you how to make chin chin the praise way

Ingredients

This are the ingredients you will need

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Nutmeg
  • Milk
  • Egg

Step by step to make chin chin

  1. First thing you need to do is to mix are your dry ingredients in one bowl
  2. After that grab a differnt bowl and mix the egg an oil and milk in that bowl
  3. Mix everything together and you should get a dough
  4. Roll your dough and cut it into small piece and start frying
